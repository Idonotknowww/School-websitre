Resilience/Prep

Emergency Grab-Bag Checklist

  • At least 3 liters of water per person (Recomended to get more)
  • Battery-powered radio and extra batteries (To get updates over radio and so you can contact for help)
  • First aid kit and essential medicines (Incase you are unable to get medical help)
  • Waterproof torch (so you still have lighting even if your torch gets wet)

3 Steps to Secure Your Home

  1. Trim high tree branches near your roof. (So the winds don't make them fall on your house.)
  2. Clear all gutters of leaves and debris. (To Reduce flooding and drain blockage.)
  3. Tape or board up large glass windows. (So the window is more secure and reinforced.)

Several Free ways you can prepare

  1. Stock up on fresh water, such as from your tap.
  2. Manage your Yard Debris ASAP to reduce damage and pollution.
  3. Create a plan of action with Neighbors and your Communtiy.
  4. Stockpile on prescription medication so you don't run out.
  5. Make a Plan and gather suppllies for cooking and other essentials without Power and Electricity.
  6. Seal Essintial things in plastic if you can such as bags of prescription medication.
  7. Stock up on food that will last without special containment such as canned food.
